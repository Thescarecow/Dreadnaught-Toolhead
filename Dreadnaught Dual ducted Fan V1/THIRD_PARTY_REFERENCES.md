# Third-Party Compatibility Targets

The Dreadnaught Toolhead is designed to interface with several third-party components. These components are compatibility targets only. Their CAD, printable files, documentation, trademarks, and project materials are not included in this repository unless expressly stated otherwise.

The Dreadnaught Toolhead licence applies only to Dreadnaught-authored material. Users requiring third-party files must obtain them from the relevant original source and comply with the applicable third-party licence or terms.

## Dreadnaught-authored compatibility parts

Some Dreadnaught-authored STL or CAD files may be named after a supported third-party component, for example an Orbiter-compatible brace or a Sherpa-compatible mounting feature.

Those files are Dreadnaught-authored compatibility or mounting parts unless expressly stated otherwise. Their name identifies the third-party component they are designed to interface with. It does not mean that the third-party component, third-party CAD, third-party printable files, or third-party source files are included.
